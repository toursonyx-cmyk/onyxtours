About Us

Luxury, reliable executive travel with comfort, privacy, and professionalism.

Welcome to Onyx Shuttle and Tour Services, where luxury meets reliability. We provide a premium, executive transport experience across Cape Town and the Garden Route, designed for clients who value comfort, privacy, and professionalism.

From airport transfers and corporate chauffeur services to hotel transfers, events, long-distance travel, and private tours, every journey is tailored to your schedule and delivered with precision.

Our focus is on punctuality, discretion, and exceptional service, ensuring a seamless experience from pickup to drop-off.

At Onyx, we understand that private travel is about more than getting from point A to point B - it is about travelling in comfort and arriving with confidence. With immaculate vehicles, professional drivers, and personalised attention, we offer a refined service you can trust every time.
